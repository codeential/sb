About Our School

Curriculum & Student Development

S. B. Memorial School is committed to holistic education that nurtures intellectual growth, physical development, cultural awareness, and creative thinking to prepare students for real-world challenges.

Academics

The school runs from Nursery to Class 8 and is affiliated with the Himachal Board of School Education, Nahan (Code: DEE 00113). The prescribed syllabus is enriched with supplementary books to enhance value-based learning and academic excellence.

Sports

Sports are given equal importance alongside academics. The school follows a House System from Class 2 onwards — Shaurya (Saffron), Shanti (White), Samridhi (Green) and Pragiti (Blue) — to promote teamwork and active participation.

Cultural Activities

Cultural and social activities help students build confidence, strengthen personal skills, and engage in enriching experiences, enabling talented students to grow and excel in their areas of interest.

Creative Learning

Even in a rural setup, strong emphasis is placed on creative education that encourages imagination, critical thinking, independence, and flexible problem-solving so students learn to think beyond the classroom.

Our Mission

To provide child-centric, value-based education that nurtures the hand, head, and heart of every learner for holistic development.

Our Vision

To nurture vibrant students and responsible citizens capable of meeting the challenges of the modern digital world.

Core Values

Holistic growth, discipline, creativity, participation, and strong moral values remain the foundation of all teaching and learning practices at our school.

S. B. Memorial School

Andheri, H.P. — An Institution of Aagosh Trust

S. B. Memorial School is a child-friendly English medium school in a remote hilly village of Himachal Pradesh, committed to holistic development, value-based learning, and equal opportunity for every child.

Vision

To provide quality, creative education to children in this remote part of India and equip them to become responsible citizens who contribute to nation building.

Mission

Provide equal opportunity to all children irrespective of caste, creed or gender through quality value education, nurturing their talents, confidence and determination to become responsible citizens.

Core Values

Inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ, we promote love, kindness, endurance, perseverance, equality and truth in all aspects of school life.

About Us

Supported by Aagosh Trust, the school has grown from Nursery–Class 5 to Nursery–Class 8 and now serves 144 students with 13 staff members. The school is affiliated to the Himachal Board of School Education and focuses strongly on child protection, wellbeing, and joyful learning.
